Jeffrey Launches Betting Website and Taunts Rival Gus Ahead of Lonely Mountain Ultra

Trail running’s fiercest rivalry takes another twist as Jeffrey unveils TrailBets.

By Sports Reporter

Trail running’s fiercest rivalry took another twist this week after Jeffrey used Strava to unveil his new betting website, TrailBets — and immediately fired a shot at long-time rival Gus.

The site lists Jeffrey the $1.72 favourite for this weekend’s Lonely Mountain Ultra 50km, with Gus the narrow outsider at $1.92. Predicted finish times on the site suggest Jeffrey will clock 5:07:34, five minutes clear of Gus’s projected 5:12:11.

“The numbers don’t lie. Five minutes clear. Lonely Mountain is mine.” — Jeffrey

When approached for comment, Gus lived up to his reputation as the sport’s humble role model. Rather than bite back, he praised his rival’s entrepreneurial move:

“Jeffrey’s always thinking big—it’s great to see trail running showcased in new ways. The predicted times look fair enough, and it should be a brilliant race.” — Gus

Then, in classic Gus fashion, he added with a grin:

“I have to admit, I like the odds boost on Mitch’s predicted time. He’s been training hard and deserves a bit of the spotlight too.” — Gus

With the launch of TrailBets adding fuel to the fire, Saturday’s race is shaping into more than just a test of endurance—it’s a battle of pride, personality, and now, the punters’ wallets.
